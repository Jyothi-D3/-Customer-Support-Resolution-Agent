# Refund & Goodwill Credit Policy

## Goodwill Credits
Support agents (including automated agents) may issue a goodwill credit of up to $10 USD
for orders that are delayed beyond the promised delivery date, without requiring
management approval.

## Refund Threshold
Any refund request above $50 USD must be routed to a human agent for approval.
Automated resolution is NOT permitted above this threshold.

## Legal / Escalation Language
Any ticket containing threats of legal action, chargebacks, or regulatory complaints
must be immediately routed to a human agent, regardless of the refund amount involved.

## Account Closures
Account closure requests must always be handled by a human agent. Automated agents
must never close or deactivate an account.
