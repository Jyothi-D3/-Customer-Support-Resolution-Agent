# Frequently Asked Questions

## Password Reset
Customers can reset their password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page.
A reset link is emailed and expires after 30 minutes.

## Account Recovery
If a customer has lost access to their recovery email, they must be routed to a
human agent for identity verification. Automated agents cannot verify identity.

## Order Status
Order status can be checked using the order ID. Statuses include: processing,
in_transit, delayed, delivered, returned.

## Out of Scope
This support agent only handles order status, returns/refunds, and account/password
help. It does not provide product comparisons, opinions on competitors, or advice
unrelated to the customer's account/order.
